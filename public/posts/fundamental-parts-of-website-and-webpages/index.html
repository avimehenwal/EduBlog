Fundamental Parts of a Webpage

29 Mar 2019
myLearning development static web
@Avi Mehenwal

The whole purpose of the existence of any webpage should be deliver the information . The type of information decides the genre of the blog post.

While the content and information presented on webpage forms the solid foundation of knowledge, presentation is equally important.

Basic components of a webpage

  1. Descriptive Header

  2. Main purpose in body

    1. Multimedia formats can help present the information more intuitive way.

      1. Audio, videos

      2. Tables, charts, graphs , historical data

      3. Text typography and font families

      4. Images and graphics

    2. structure of the content

      1. Introduction and abstract with real-life problem or motivation

      2. Suggested solution(s)

      3. Comparison with existing solution. Making a case for better solution

      4. Data to support claim

      5. Conclusion with call to action

      6. References and bibliography

  3. sidebars

    1. advertisements

    2. promotional / take action step

  4. Footer

    1. Website navigation, site map

    2. Legals and copyrights

  1. Be creative and add extra layer of fun around the content.

  2. Minimalist design

  3. Follow through conventions
